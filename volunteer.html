Why Volunteer With Us?

RIGGI Rescue relies on compassionate volunteers to help with dog walking, fostering, event planning, transportation, fundraising, and more. Volunteering is a rewarding experience that directly impacts the lives of our rescue dogs!

  • Walk and socialize dogs
  • Assist with adoption events
  • Help with administrative tasks
  • Provide temporary foster homes
  • Transport dogs to appointments
