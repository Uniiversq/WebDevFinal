Learn More About Fostering

Everything you need to know about becoming a foster hero.

What Does a Foster Do?

Foster families play a critical role in helping dogs transition from shelter life to a forever home. You’ll provide temporary care, love, and socialization, helping us learn more about each dog’s personality and needs.

What We Provide

  • Food, treats, and necessary supplies
  • All medical care and vet visits
  • Support and training tips

What You Provide

  • A safe, loving home environment
  • Basic training and socialization
  • Transportation to vet appointments or adoption events (if possible)

How Long Is the Commitment?

Most fosters last from a few weeks to a few months, depending on the dog’s needs. We work with you to match dogs that fit your lifestyle and availability.

Can I Adopt My Foster?

Absolutely! Many fosters fall in love and choose to adopt — we call it a “foster fail,” and we love happy endings!
