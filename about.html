About RIGGI Rescue

Our story, our mission, and the hearts behind the rescue.

Who We Are

RIGGI Rescue is a passionate, volunteer-driven dog rescue organization dedicated to saving, rehabilitating, and rehoming homeless dogs. Founded in 2010, we have saved hundreds of lives and matched dogs with loving families across our community.

Our Mission

We believe every dog deserves a second chance. Our mission is to rescue dogs in need, provide them with quality medical care, foster them in loving homes, and match them with forever families. We also strive to educate the public about responsible pet ownership and the importance of spaying and neutering.

Our Team

RIGGI Rescue is powered by a dedicated team of volunteers, fosters, veterinarians, and donors. Without their hard work and compassion, none of this would be possible. Together, we create a lifeline for dogs who have no other hope.
